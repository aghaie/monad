# Q6 — Omission Architecture Report (Phase ΩΣ)

**Method:** `foundational-questions-1.0` · corpus-only · deterministic.

## 1. Objective
What does the Quran systematically leave unsaid — information gaps, unresolved references, structural
silence?

## 2. Method
Structural-silence proxy: pronominal/anaphoric load relative to content nouns — a high PRON-to-N ratio
indicates reference resolved outside the local ayah. Counts are exact POS censuses.

## 3. Results
- PRON tokens: **3,301** · N tokens: **25,136** · PRON-to-N ratio ≈ **0.131**.
- Substantial pronominal reference is left to resolution beyond the local ayah; the *content* of what is
  omitted is not recoverable.

## 4. Interpretation
Omission is **structurally real** — there is a measurable anaphoric load whose antecedents lie outside the
immediate context (an "omission architecture" in the weak sense). But *what* is omitted is semantic and
unrecoverable. So the architecture of silence is detectable; its content is not.

## 5. Falsification Attempts
The PRON-to-N ratio is an exact census. It demonstrates anaphora exists but cannot be pushed to a content
claim.

## 6. Limitations
Anaphora resolution and discourse-level gap detection would require parsing beyond the available
representation; only the aggregate ratio is reported.

## 7. Conclusion
**PARTIAL.** Omission is structurally present (anaphora, ratio ≈ 0.13 pronouns per noun), but its content is
UNKNOWN.
