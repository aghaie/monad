## 4. Interpretation
With all of Monad's current knowledge, **~20% of the per-root structure of the Quran is
explained — entirely by lexical frequency — and ~80% is unexplained.** The discovered
relational structure (proposition network, motifs, grammar, semantics) adds real *description*
but no generalizable *explanation* beyond frequency. The frontier sits where frequency stops.

## 5. Falsification Attempts
The frontier rests on the maximum-model measurement (B/D) and the redundancy result (C); both
were attacked and both hold. No layer pushes the frontier past 20%.

## 6. Limitations
The 20/80 split is for the per-root operationalization; a different target would change the
absolute split but not the conclusion that explanation saturates at frequency.

## 7. Conclusion
**Explanation frontier: ~20% explained (frequency), ~80% unexplained.** This is the measured
boundary of the project's current knowledge.
